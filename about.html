Chetan

Deepak

Lyricist & Creative Writer

As a lyricist and music producer, I've spent my life figuring out how to make people feel something within the first few seconds of a track. Now, I am taking that same obsession with pacing and emotion and bringing it to the screen. Transitioning from the music industry to screenwriting feels like a natural evolution—it is all about crafting the perfect hook, building tension, and telling a story that stays with you long after the final scene.
